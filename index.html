About Us

About Us

Ramanthapur Dental Clinic is the oldest continuing dental practice in Ramanthapur. Established in 2005 our dental office has been providing quality dental care to Hyderabad patients for over a decade. This legacy of treatment comes with responsibility. A responsibility to treat people with respect, excellence, and compassion..


If you have any questions or comments regarding any of the dental work our dentists perform, please feel free to give us a call or contact us via the web. One of our friendly associates will be more than happy to assist you. Remember, if you want quality dental work performed by experienced and knowledgeable dentists, then Ramanthapur Multi Speciality Dental Clinic is the place you've been looking for!

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Srinivas N.C.H Cheif Medical Officer

Dr. Srinivas N.C.H is a first-generation dentist who began his dental career around age 12. Watching his mothers compassion, expertise and professionalism gave him inspiration. Post-graduation, he started practice in Ramanthapur, Hyderabad, where he was born and raised.

Dr. Srinivas received his MDS degree from the Medical College of Virginia (VCU School of Dentistry). He continues to pursue advanced post-doctoral training in individual dental specialties including dental implants, smile design, temporomandibular joint disorder (TMD), sleep apnea, and Invisalign® orthodontics.

Dr. Srinivas’ accolades include being named top 40 Under 40 by Incisal Edge Magazine, Premiere Invisalign Gold provider 2017, Open Care Practice of the Year 2012, Best of Yelp (2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018), Google 5-Star Provider, Angie’s List Grade A Provider, and numerous community philanthropic awards.

Dr. Srinivas has one mission is to provide the highest standard of care to his patients through time spent with each of them, advanced technology and techniques, and a genuinely caring and compassionate orientation toward patient care. He is especially adept at making even the biggest “dental coward” feel like they are at a relaxing spa. If you are a person who has had difficulty getting numb in the past, give Dr. Srinivas N.C.H a try and enjoy being numb for once!

He is an active member of the Indian Dental Association (IDA), the Hyderabad Dental Association (HDA), and the Telangana Dental Association (TDA). He is also a long-time volunteer dentist for Missions of Mercy (MOM), which provides free dental care to underprivileged families.
